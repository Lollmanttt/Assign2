The Shire

The Shire

So... You found yourself stuck in the shire, alone and possibly afraid? maybe not but still this guide should be useful to you.

First thing you're going to want to do is find whereabouts you are, honestly you should be fine as long as you dont wander into a random forest or try to feed the wildlife. Incase you feel lost and are to scared to camp out, find one of the small Hobbit towns and you should be pretty safe there, they are pretty recognizable by the round doors and windows found in every Hobbit home.

After you find a place to stay the next good step is to find a place to get some food, though that shouldnt be too hard with hobbits having 7 meals a day and loving all sorts of food.
